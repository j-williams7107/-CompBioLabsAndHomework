Ingredients
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp hot water
1/2 tsp salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions
Preheat oven to 350F
Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips. Place large spoonfuls onto ungreased pan.
Bake for about 10 minutes in preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
